The Impact of Electricity

The methods companies use to generate electricity and which kind of generation you choose are the deciding factors of your envrionmental impact from electricity.


Environmental impact from electricity can be measured using 3 factors; how much electricity is generated, electricity generation techniques used by the companies providing the power, and the air pollution devices used by those same companies. Most electricity right now is made from non-renewable resources.


There are a few ways to reduce our environemental impact through our food choices, such as:

  • Switching to more energy-efficient method to generate electricity

  • Using a bit of solar energy

  • Choosing to buy more locally grown produce

  • Eat fewer foods that use lots of water to produce, like tea, beef, chocolate (cocoa tree), dairy products, rice, etc.

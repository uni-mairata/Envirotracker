The Impact of Water

Our use of water impacts the Earth, and the Earth's supply of available fresh water impacts us.

Only 1.2% of the 3% of the Earth's freshwater source is available to us. Having very easy access to drinking water like in the United States is a reality that most people don't experience, especially in areas where water is scarce from the starts.

However, most people dont' realize this in the U.S., and a majority of the population tends to waste this preciuos freshwater without realizing the impacts. And because of this careless use, even citizens in the United States are experiencing water shortages. Several areas of the United States are facing water shortages that can be detrimental and spread to other regions which is seen in the image below. This makes conserving water increasingly important.

There are a few ways to help conserve water in our everyday lives, such as:

  • Showering for a shorter amount of time

  • Making sure the faucet is off when brushing your teeth

  • Don't use excess water when washing dishes

  • Eat fewer foods that use lots of water to produce, like tea, beef, chocolate (cocoa tree), dairy products, rice, etc.
